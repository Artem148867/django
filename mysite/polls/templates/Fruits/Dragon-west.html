Dragon Fruit (West)

The Dragon Fruit allows the user to gain the power, and transform to a Mythical Dragon. The user can transform either to an Eastern Dragon; resembling those from the Chinese culture, or the Western Dragon; resembling an medieval armored Dragon from the European folklore. The user can also transform into a large, tall human-hybrid dragon that deals more massive fiery damage.
