Yeti fruit

Kitsune grants the user the ability to conjure and manipulate blue fire into destructive attacks and allows them to transform into a creature in Japanese mythology, the Kitsune, enhancing their attacks significantly, and many abilities such as speed and the ability to walk on water, at the cost of some of their tails.

Transform

The user gets a speed boost +100% [2x] and a dash boost and now runs on all fours. (Air jumping, dashing, and walking will also now have a unique animation)

M1

Normal Attack

The user strikes with a pair of ice claws twice, followed by a stomp on the third one, and then unleashes a roar that can knock the enemy backwards on the final one.

Human Transform

Z skill

Arctic Clap

The user's hand will attract icy energy and cause the screen to shake a little. When released, the user compiles a large amount of snow on their hands and slams them together, causing a straight-line explosion of ice, knocking away enemies a good distance. This ability cannot be aimed vertically, and has quite a small range. When used in the air, this attack will appear as a powerful gust of cold wind instead.

Z skill

Arctic Clap

The user attracts even more energy to their hands, causing the screen to tremble, before launching a bigger straight-line ice explosion that knocks targets backwards, albeit with ice spikes all over the floor pointing towards the cursor, similar to Awakened Ice's X move. When used on water, it will create ice blocks underneath. In the air, it will create an even bigger burst of wind from a cold storm.

Human Transform

X skill

Frostbite Boulder

The user formulates a huge snowball from their hand. When released, the user hurls the snowball towards the cursor, exploding after crashing into any surface, damaging enemies caught in the impact with a snowy blast while leaving traces of snow behind. Unlike other ball-shaped moves such as Rumble's and Flame's V, the speed of which the snowball is thrown is extremely fast, akin to a bullet. When the snowball lands in water, it will create an ice platform at where it landed.

X skill

Frostbite Boulder

The user hurls a massive snowball that, upon landing, splits into smaller snowballs, damaging enemies within the impact. When used on water, it will create a large ice block at where it landed, making a temporary platform for teammates to stand on while fighting Sea Events. The large ice block created by the snowball can stop enemy boats in its tracks, and the same goes for the transformed's frostwalk passive.

Human Transform

С skill

Glacial Execution

The user launches themselves towards the cursor, and upon contact, they exhale a breathe of freezing air that encases the opponent, before dashing to behind them and blasting them forwards with a devastating amount of force. This move is single target, so it is not recommended to use this for grinding. When aimed towards water, a small ice platform will form in the water where the user is supposed to land.

C skill

Glacial Execution

The user hurls a massive snowball that, upon landing, splits into smaller snowballs, damaging enemies within the impact. When used on water, it will create a large ice block at where it landed, making a temporary platform for teammates to stand on while fighting Sea Events. The large ice block created by the snowball can stop enemy boats in its tracks, and the same goes for the transformed's frostwalk passive.

Human Transform

V skill

Transformation

The user roars, causing ice spikes to pierce out of the ground, dealing damage and knockback to any nearby enemies, before transforming into an upright, huge Yeti with red, glowing eyes. The user gains the ability to walk on water, but loses the ability to use Fighting Styles, Swords and Guns. The size of the Yeti transformation is about as tall as a transformed T-Rex.

V skill

Transformation

The user transforms back into their normal form, allowing the user to use fighting styles, swords and guns. Keep in mind that the user also loses the ability to walk on water by doing this.

Fly Dragon

F skill

Tundra Rush

The user charges and grabs the enemy, slamming them into the ground three times, creating ice explosions and ice ground spikes on the floor. In the air, the user makes the enemy spin and then throws the enemy away. Just like the [C] move, Glacial Execution, this move is single target.

F skill

Tundra Rush

The user charges and grabs the enemy, slamming them onto the ground three times and then slamming them backwards. If used in the air, user creates an ice boulder around the target, slams into the ground and then throws them.
