Kitsune fruit

Kitsune grants the user the ability to conjure and manipulate blue fire into destructive attacks and allows them to transform into a creature in Japanese mythology, the Kitsune, enhancing their attacks significantly, and many abilities such as speed and the ability to walk on water, at the cost of some of their tails.

2 tails form

The user gets a speed boost +100% [2x] and a dash boost and now runs on all fours. (Air jumping, dashing, and walking will also now have a unique animation)

3 tails form

The user gets three glowing orbs, when using [F] move or an M1, the enemy will deal tick damage by blue flames.

Transform

While transformed, the user gains a 20% damage reduction from all sources, improved attacks, faster speed, jump twice higher than average, and increased dash distance. When transformed, the user gains the ability to run on Water, but they are not able to idle on it. When transformed, the user can be mounted by another allied player, riding their saddle.

Human Transform

M1 skill

Normal Attack

The user slashes the air, creating blue flame slashes. On the fourth slash, the user swipes in a whirlwind style. And with the last slash having two variations, on the ground the user swipes downwards, leaving a faint crack for a few moments, in the air the user kicks the opponent to the ground (Both variations deal the same amount of damage). Once at 3 tails, the slashes will inflict ticking damage and the opponent will be covered in blue flame for between 4-5 seconds and the base damage will increase too. Every slash drains the Tails meter by a bit. Jumping right before the slam will make the user perform a slash instead of a slam.

Human Transform

Z skill

Accursed Enchantment

If hit: The user summons blue fire flames that circle the opponent for some time before hitting the opponent multiple times, dealing damage.

If not hit: The user creates a small AoE that deals low damage to nearby targets.

Z skill

Accursed Enchantment

If hit: Targets up to 1-3 opponents, launching them backward and shooting a ball of blue flames per target (untransformed C is used), causing an explosion of blue flames as well as ticking damage.

If not hit: The user will spin and launch a flurry of blue fireballs around them, causing a large AoE explosion.

Human Transform

X skill

Tails of Burning Agony

This move does damage in a zig-zag shape, first hitting one corner, then hitting another, and finally hitting the target. This move can hit up to three targets and stun the enemy for ~0.65s.

X skill

Tails of Burning Agony

The user dashes forward in a zig-zag shape. If hits, the user will grab the enemy, carry them into the air, and then slam them to the ground hard. This move will break Instinct and blue flames will then rise from the ground along with several large tails. This move instantly slams an opponent if used in air. The face of a Kitsune will be printed on the ground when hit.

Human Transform

С skill

Fox Fire Disruption

The user summons a ball of blue flames and strikes it at the enemy when the key is released. The ball of flames will erupt into a huge explosion, and flames will be present if landed near the ground. These break Instinct. The move deals slightly more damage if it is a direct hit.

С skill

Fox Fire Disruption

The user launches a blue fireball and causes widespread damage. After impact, there will be blue flames that cause extremely large damage over time.
